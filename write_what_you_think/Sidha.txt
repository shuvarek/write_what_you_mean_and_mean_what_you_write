Sidha
Sandstone, Uttar Pradesh, 1000-1100
The goddess Siddha a form of Gauri, holds lotus blosoms oin her upper hands. She is associated with the iguana seen on the base below her feet.
EA1999-21


India and Kashmir 600 - 1900


===
In the room 32 at Asholean museum which cover Indian art from years 600 to 1900 an interesting sculpture can be found. It is goddess Sidha which reminds me mother earth, full of love and devoted to care about her children which her big breast and hands symbolise well.
